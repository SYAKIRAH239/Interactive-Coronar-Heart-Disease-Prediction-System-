Features

Everything you need for
modern clinical AI

Heartfy combines cutting-edge machine learning with a human-first approach — so every prediction is accurate, transparent, and clinically meaningful.

01
Stacked Ensemble Model

Combines Random Forest, XGBoost and Logistic Regression into a powerful stacking classifier for higher accuracy than any single model alone.

02
SHAP Explainability

Every prediction is accompanied by a SHAP waterfall chart showing exactly which clinical factors drove the result — no black box.

03
Gemini AI Explanation

Google Gemini reads the SHAP chart and translates it into plain clinical language — giving doctors an instant narrative explanation they can act on.

04
Scenario Analysis

Adjust clinical parameters interactively with sliders and see how the predicted risk changes in real time — ideal for patient consultations.

05
PDF Clinical Reports

Generate professional, downloadable PDF reports for each prediction — including patient data, SHAP chart, and AI explanation.

06
Dataset Dashboard

Explore interactive visualizations of the 5,449-patient training dataset — cholesterol, BMI, demographics, and CHD distribution.
